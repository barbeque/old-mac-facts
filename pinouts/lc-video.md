# LC video pinout
| LC Pin | Signal                 |
|--------|------------------------|
|1	 | Red ground |
|2	 | Red video signal |
|5	 | Green video signal |
|6	 | Green ground |
|9	 | Blue video signal |
|13	 | Blue ground |
|15	 | /HSYNC |
|12	 | /VSYNC |
|14	 | HSYNC ground |
|7, 10	 | SENSE1 & SENSE2 tied together ("VGA") |
